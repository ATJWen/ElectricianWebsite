Who we are

D-Lectronics unofficialy begun in 2003, when co-founders Daragh and Darragh met in their late university days as housemates. With a passion for electronics, they started a project helping to repair electrical appliances ranging from landline telephones to refrigerators to earn some money on the side. After graduating with a Masters from Sheffield Hallam University, Daragh and Darragh officially launched D-lectronics.

Since 2006, the company has made it's way into the lives of the Irish people. Here at D-Lectronics we specialise in installing televisions, aerials, satellite systems and wireless communications. We always comply with state-of-the-art technology and standards.
